About James Greenwood

Welcome to my personal site.

I’m the founder of CognitiveInsight.ai, where I build AI tools that help people think clearly, reflect deeply, and act meaningfully.

My work centers on AI-Augmented Thinking: the use of AI not to replace human insight, but to enhance self-awareness, strategic clarity, and identity-aligned action. I design systems that support emotional resilience, structured decision-making, and cognitive reframing in high-stakes or uncertain moments.

Through frameworks like The Cognitive Edge Protocol, I guide users from overwhelm to opportunity—using conversational AI as a catalyst for personal transformation.

"AI should help you hear your own thoughts more clearly, not drown them out."

Thanks for stopping by. Let’s explore what better thinking can unlock—for you, your work, and the future we’re shaping.
